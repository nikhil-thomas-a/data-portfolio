# Findings — Startup KPI Dashboard

## The short version

A B2B SaaS startup growing from £12k to £60k MRR over 24 months.
NRR averaging 106% — expansion revenue outpacing churn.
LTVCAC of 22x is well above the 3x minimum benchmark.
CAC declining over time as marketing efficiency improves.

## Key observations

Revenue MRR grew 368% over 24 months. Growth accelerated in
months 8-16 (the classic S-curve for early SaaS).

Retention NRR above 100% every month from month 6 onwards.
This means existing customers are expanding faster than others churn —
the single most important signal of product-market fit.

Unit economics LTVCAC peaked at 32x and averaged 22x.
Payback period dropped from ~3 months to ~2 months as CAC declined.
Both are strong signals of a capital-efficient acquisition model.

Engagement DAUMAU ratio held at ~38% throughout — healthy for
a B2B tool. Feature adoption grew steadily from 45% to 85%.

Support CSAT stayed above 4.05 while ticket volume grew with
the customer base — response time improved, suggesting the support
function scaled efficiently.
